Rooms

Standard Room

A simple, practical room option for guests who want essential comfort throughout the stay.

  • Bathroom and WC
  • Simple residential setup
  • Close to the program flow

Comfort Plus Room

A balanced choice for guests looking for a slightly more spacious stay.

  • More spacious layout
  • Rest-focused furniture
  • Practical access to daily routines

Suite Room

For guests planning a longer program or preferring more personal space.

  • Separate lounge feeling
  • Suited for longer stays
  • More personal space

Private Terrace Floor

Considered during consultation for guests who prefer a calmer, more private room experience.

  • Terrace use
  • A calmer stay feeling
  • Availability checked during consultation

Deluxe Family Room

Can be planned for guests joining the program with family members or companions.

  • For families or guests arriving together
  • Larger usable area
  • Guest count clarified in consultation

Bungalow / Wooden House

A prominent option for guests seeking a more nature-connected stay.

  • Closer to nature
  • More detached room feeling
  • Availability shared during consultation

Room options and availability are shared during pre-consultation.
